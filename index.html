Sportsbook New User Arbitrage

Sportsbooks frequently offer new users a refund for their first losing bet, however, this refund is in the form of site credit. This credit (equal to the amount of the losing wager up to a specified limit), is not withdrawable until it has been "played through" or "rolled over".

This means that when a sportsbook offers a "risk-free bet up to $1,000" and your $1,000 bet loses, then you would get $1,000 in site credit that you then have to wager again. Additionally, site credit does not payout in the same manner as a normal wager. Since you are "staking" $1,000 of credit (i.e. their money, not yours), then you only receive the winnings from the credited bet. Essentially, if you win your credited bet you are still at risk of losing money!
